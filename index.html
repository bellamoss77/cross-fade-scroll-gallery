May Days

Central Virginia, 2024

The world comes alive

Explosions of greenery and gold sooth the eyes.

Bright blooms and broken trinkets

blend together seamlessly in a cacaphony of color.

Radiant reds...

infuse bursts of life - both flora and fauna.

Springtime rainstorms replenish the river

infused with virginia red clay, it evinces an energy that calls out to sweet creatures.

This routine radiant rebirth

tempts all to enjoy the outdoors.
